Too Hipster for Instagram

How to be too hipster for instagram:

Captions must be in terms that normal people cannot understand. Normal people are all such peasants.

Text must be right justified and randomly placed to make one ponder about life. And it's totes chic.

All photographs must be faded using low contrast and low saturation. They should look slightly like a polaroid.

Instagram is too mainstream. Go bike to a coffee shop with your little rucksack and code your own website.

And then you get so far from mainstream your photos aren't even square. Look at what a rebel you've become.
